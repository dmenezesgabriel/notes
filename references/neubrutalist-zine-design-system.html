NOTES / PKM / SECOND-BRAIN
On building a second brain
MAY 2026 · 8 MIN READ · PKM
The goal isn't to remember everything — it's to think better by offloading memory to a trusted external system. Your brain is for generating ideas, not for storing them indefinitely.
"We are what we repeatedly do. Excellence, then, is not an act, but a habit." — Aristotle
The capture habit
Every note you capture is a future conversation with yourself. The format matters less than the habit of externalising thinking before it disappears.
✦ TIP Start with a weekly review — fifteen minutes every Sunday is enough to close the loop and trust the system.
→ RELATED Zettelkasten, evergreen notes, PARA method, progressive summarisation.
